Its me Black Box investigation

By Adham

Background

In Mr. Sinuses class we were tasked to figure out what is inside this box called the Mystery Black box. The mystery black box is a black box that has a funnel and tube coming out of it. When we pour water into the funnel it may come out sometimes but sometimes it doesn't come out. In the New media and tech class we were challenged to design one without knowing what is inside the real one to recreate the effect of getting more less and none sometimes.

Claim

I believe there is tubing and a box to store extra water that's not needed or doesn't flow out to add the effect of extra water coming out.

Evidence

Our evidence was that it had to have space to store water. Because when we poured water into it the first few times it wouldn't have water coming out but, then sometimes we had more water come out and even less, (look at table 1) when sometimes we poured in 880mL and got 890mL out. In our black box we put a box inside our black box to store water while we pour it and so it doesn't come out always and store water for a later time without leaking minimal sound and so sometimes more comes out during the pour.

Reasing, Part1

Models are representations of a design to show/ demo something or prove something. Models are useful because they help you imagine different ideas that are possible and disprove some. Limits of a model would be that they aren't made from the same material as the actual product but also are similar and strengths of a model is a way to try different ideas.

Reasoning, Part2

The evidence I have supports my claim that there is a bucket to store extra water. Because it shows why sometimes more or less water comes out when you pour water in.

Reasoning, Part3

Another idea people believe in is the tipping bucket but then that doesn't make sense because if there is a tipping bucket then once it tips over it would tip back again and stay over and the water will go everywhere.
